제5조 (개인정보의 제3자 제공)

클라우드 서버 등을 통한 국외 저장 가능, 관련 법령 준수

Article 1 (Purpose)

This policy outlines how 3O LLC ("Company") collects, uses, retains, and disposes of personal information of users of the FFLIK FOOD service.

Article 2 (Collected Information)

  • Required: Name, phone number, social login info, address
  • Optional: Special order requests or additional input fields

Article 3 (Purpose of Use)

  • To provide food ordering and delivery mediation services
  • To verify identity and respond to inquiries
  • To improve services and analyze usage statistics

Article 4 (Retention Period)

  • Until membership withdrawal
  • Or longer as required by applicable laws

Article 5 (Third-party Provision)

  • Information provided only to restaurants/couriers for delivery
  • Or when required by law

Article 6 (Overseas Transfer)

Personal information may be stored abroad via cloud servers; legal compliance ensured

Article 7 (Destruction Procedure and Method)

  • Deleted without delay upon fulfillment of purpose or expiration
  • Digital data deleted irrecoverably; paper records shredded or incinerated

Article 8 (User Rights)

  • May request access, correction, or deletion of personal data
  • May withdraw consent; services may be limited as a result

Article 9 (Privacy Officer)

  • Officer: 김명환
  • Email: official@threeo.info
